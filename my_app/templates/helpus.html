Pilot Volunteer Sign-Up

What to Expect as a Pilot

  • Guiding and Assisting: Ensure a safe and enjoyable cycling experience for our users by providing guidance and support during rides.
  • On or Off-Road Rides: Participate in both on-road and off-road cycling sessions, tailored to the preferences and abilities of the user.
  • Regular Commitment: Commit to regular cycling sessions to provide consistent support and build trust with our users.
  • Safety and Training: Attend training sessions to learn best practices for tandem cycling and ensure the safety of both you and your cycling partner.

Benefits of Being a Pilot

  • Personal Fulfillment: Experience the joy and satisfaction of making a positive impact on someone's life by helping them enjoy the freedom and benefits of cycling.
  • Build Connections: Form meaningful relationships with users and other volunteers within our supportive community.
  • Stay Active: Enjoy regular physical activity and improve your own fitness while cycling with a purpose.
  • Learn and Grow: Gain valuable skills and knowledge through training sessions and hands-on experience in tandem cycling.

Ready to make a difference? Sign up to become a pilot volunteer using the registration form below. Join us in our mission to empower individuals through the joy of cycling!

Why Your Donations Matter

At Freedom Wheels, donations are the lifeblood of our mission. They enable us to provide essential support and services to individuals with Parkinson's and visual impairments. Here's how your contributions make a difference:

  • Equipment and Maintenance: Your donations help us acquire and maintain high-quality tandem bikes and other specialized equipment, ensuring safe and enjoyable rides for all our users.
  • Volunteer Support: We rely on dedicated volunteers to guide and assist our users. Donations help us provide training, support, and resources to our volunteer pilots, ensuring they are well-prepared and motivated.
  • Events: Hosting events is a crucial part of building our community. Donations enable us to organize inclusive and engaging activities that foster connections and provide valuable experiences for our users.
  • Website and Resources: Maintaining an accessible and informative website is vital for our community. Your contributions help us keep our website up-to-date, provide valuable resources, and ensure seamless user experiences.

Become a Volunteer Fundraiser

If you're passionate about our mission but prefer not to pilot a tandem bike, you can still make a significant impact as a volunteer fundraiser. Here's what being a volunteer fundraiser involves:

  • Organizing Events: Plan and execute fundraising events, such as charity rides, bake sales, and community gatherings, to raise awareness and funds for our cause.
  • Community Outreach: Engage with the community to spread the word about Freedom Wheels, encouraging others to support our mission through donations and participation.
  • Campaign Management: Help manage fundraising campaigns, both online and offline, to maximize our reach and impact.

Benefits of Being a Volunteer Fundraiser

  • Personal Satisfaction: Experience the joy of knowing you're making a tangible difference in the lives of individuals with disabilities.
  • Skill Development: Gain valuable skills in event planning, communication, and campaign management.
  • Build Connections: Form meaningful relationships with other volunteers and community members, creating a supportive network.

Interested in volunteering as a fundraiser? Complete the volunteer pilot form and specify that you'd like to help with fundraising. Together, we can make a difference and bring the joy of cycling to everyone.
